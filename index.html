Welcome to A World Without H8! Enjoy your Stay.

A World Without H8 is a brand based out of State College, Pennsylvania. The goal of this brand is to emphasize the fact that there is inequality in this world, and not everyone has the same socioeconomic benefits that others are given and take for granted.

With that being said, 25% of all profits will go to the charity Heart to Heart International. Heart to Heart International strengthens communities through improving health access, providing humanitarian development and administering crisis relief worldwide.

Let's make this world better, Together!
